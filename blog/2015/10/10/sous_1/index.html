Morton’s Tri-tip at 130 for 6 hours

Came out extremely tender. 7 hours might make it a little better in the middle. Cutting a 3.5 - 4 lb tri-tip in half also could help.

Seared in a cast iron skillet with oil and butter. 15 seconds per flip for two minutes.
